PMSG · Frequency Response · Virtual Inertia

Frequency Response and SFD Inhibition Methods of Wind Power Generation

Developed a MATLAB/Simulink simulation model for the grid-connected frequency response of a PMSG-based wind power system. The model included wind turbine and PMSG dynamics, a back-to-back dual-PWM full-scale converter, a DC-link stage, machine-side and grid-side converter control, and virtual inertia control.

Based on the model, I simulated frequency responses under load disturbances, analyzed the secondary frequency drop during rotor speed recovery, and evaluated an adaptive droop control strategy under different wind power penetration levels.
